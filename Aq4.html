Custom Orders FAQ

Can I customize my order?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our products. You can choose colors, sizes, and even add custom designs based on availability.

How do I provide my specifications?

To provide your custom specifications:

  1. Select the product you want to customize.
  2. Click on the "Customize" button on the product page.
  3. Fill in the required details such as size, color, and any additional requests.
  4. Upload your design or file if required.
  5. Add the customized product to your cart and proceed to checkout.

If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
